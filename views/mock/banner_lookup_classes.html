To perform a more detailed search of classes, click the Advanced Search button without selecting any subjects and follow the instructions on the Advanced Search page.

To view a list of courses, select the subject(s) and click the Course Search button. On the Class Search Results page, click the View Sections button next to the desired course to view the available sections of that course.

Firefox users: If you receive the error message “Your changes have already been submitted.”, Refresh/Reload the page and continue with your search.

Safari users on iOS 7.0.3+: Due to a current issue with iOS, in order to perform a search you will need to select at least two subjects. Please select at least two subjects and click the Course Search button. The last subject selected will not display in your search results.
